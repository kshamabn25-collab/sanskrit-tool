Everything you need for Indic scripts

⌨️

Inline IME — type in any app

A system-wide input method that intercepts your keystrokes. Type phonetic Roman and get Indic script inserted directly into any app — WhatsApp, Notion, Word, browser — with no window switching or copy-pasting.

🔄

6 scripts + IAST

Devanagari, Kannada, Telugu, Tamil, Bengali, Malayalam, and IAST — all from the same phonetic input. Switch scripts instantly with Alt+Shift+O without leaving what you're typing.

📋

Offline converter with dual output

Press Alt+Shift+I to open the full converter window. See your chosen Indic script and its IAST transliteration side by side. Copy either with one click.

⚡

Quick floating panel

Press Alt+Shift+S for a lightweight overlay — type a word, see the live preview, press Enter and it's pasted directly into whatever app is behind it.

🖥️

macOS & Windows

One app, both platforms. The inline IME, offline converter, and quick panel all work on macOS and Windows. Inline typing inserts text directly — no clipboard required.

✈️

Fully offline, always ready

Zero internet required. No accounts, no telemetry, no subscriptions. Lives in your menu bar or system tray and launches at login — always one hotkey away.
